Toast with Refried Beans and Avocado

This recipe takes 5 minutes and is sure to satisfy a hungry vegan looking for breakfast! You'll need a toaster, measuring cups and a knife.

  1. Ingredients you will need
    1. 2 slices vegan sandwich bread
    2. 1 cup vegan refried beans
    3. 1 avocado, thinly sliced
    4. A few slivers of white onion
    5. Coarse sea salt
  2. Directions
    1. Toast vegan bread.
    2. Top with refried beans.
    3. Top with avocado.
    4. Mash with a fork, if desired.
    5. Add slivered onions.
    6. Sprinkle with salt.
    Serve and enjoy your delicious vegan toast!
