Chocolate Peanut Butter Banana Shake

A perfect healthy vegan breakfast that you can take on the go! You will need a blender to mix together all of the ingredients and measuring cups/spoons.

  1. Ingredients you will need
    1. 1 banana
    2. 2 tablespoons peanut butter
    3. 1-2 dates
    4. 1 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa powder
    5. 1-1 ½ cups unsweetened almond milk
    6. (Optional) ½ cup ice
  2. Preparing the shake
    1. Add all ingredients except almond milk to the blender.
    2. Blend.
    3. Add in almond milk ½ cup at a time while ingredients blend together.Note: add more almond milk for a thinner shake and less for a thicker shake. Also, add a handful of ice for extra thickness.
    4. Taste and adjust flavors, adding more bananas or dates for sweetness, cocoa powder for chocolate, and peanut butter for saltiness.
      Enjoy your tasty Chocolate Peanut Butter Banana Shake!
